Mission:

To provide a safe, accessible, and vibrant basketball court for our barangay, promoting physical health, teamwork, and community camaraderie. We aim to nurture the love for basketball among residents of all ages while fostering values of sportsmanship, discipline, and unity.

Vision:

To create a thriving hub for sports and community interaction, where the barangay basketball court becomes a symbol of inclusivity, talent development, and lasting connections. We envision empowering the youth and bringing the community closer through the joy and spirit of basketball.
